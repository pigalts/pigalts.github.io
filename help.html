WHAT CAN I DO IF ONE OF MY PRODUCTS IS BROKEN?

Sorry about that. Please contact the Support Chat - click on the bubble in the bottom right corner and describe your problem. Support is available 24 hours a day and will help you in a few minutes.

SO WITH A LIFETIME GUARANTEE, WILL I ALWAYS GET HELP?

Correct, you always get help with your order. If you ever have a problem, just write to customer support on pigalts.com by submitting a ticket or live chat. Blazing-fast help!

MY ACCOUNT HAS BEEN COMPROMISED, WHAT DO I DO NOW?                                 

In many cases where people believe that their accounts have been stolen or compromised, the issue is instead caused by a simple login issue. First, try to change your password. Log out of anything that you're logged into (websites, game launcher), then if you were able to change the password, try to log in again.

If you are not getting password reset emails within a few minutes, or you are sure that your account was compromised, locate your Transaction ID and raise a ticket. PigAlts has the ability to restore all their "Lifetime warranty accounts" back to their owner.
